Lasagna


In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic over medium heat. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top. Spread half of the ricotta cheese, followed by a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is golden. Let it cool, garnish with fresh basil if desired, then slice and serve.
