Pizza


Create your delicious homemade pizza with the following steps:
1. Start by preparing your pizza dough. You can either make it from scratch or buy pre-made dough from the store.
2. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
3. Spread a layer of your favorite pizza sauce over the dough.
4. Add your favorite toppings such as cheese, pepperoni, veggies, and more.
5. Bake in a preheated oven according to the dough instructions or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
6. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and then slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
